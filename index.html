OUR STORY

An authentic Mexican restaurant ran by a father and daughter. You will be treated you as if you are having a meal in our home as a welcome guest. Once you try our tacos, you will continue to think about them all day and every day until you, eventually, come back for more.

We continually strive to provide the best dining experience available; therefore, should we need to improve in any area, please give us your feedback!



OUR HISTORY

Established in 2018. Everardo Macias, an immigrant from Jalisco who has resided in Chicago since the 70's, decided to open a Mexican restaurant in 2017. He put his blood, sweat and tears into renovating the restaurant into what it is today. With help from his daughter Jessica, including her modern vision of a tradition Mexican dining experience, Tacotlan was born.
